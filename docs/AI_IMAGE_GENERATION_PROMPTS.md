- **Error:** `#EF4444`
- **Warning:** `#F59E0B`

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (primary), Monaco (code blocks)
- **Headings (H1):** 36-48px, Bold (700)
- **Headings (H2):** 24-32px, Bold (700)
- **Headings (H3):** 16-18px, Bold (700)
- **Body Text:** 14px, Regular (400)
- **Small Text:** 12px, Regular (400)
- **Captions:** 11-12px, Regular (400)
- **Code/Mono:** 11-14px, Monaco Regular (400)

### Spacing Scale
2px, 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px, 40px, 60px, 80px

### Border Radius
- **Small:** 4px
- **Standard:** 6-8px (most used)
- **Medium:** 10px
- **Large:** 12px
- **Full (pills):** 9999px

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Desktop:** 1024px and above
- **Tablet:** 640px - 1023px
- **Mobile:** Below 640px
